# Thumbnail generation guidance
# Read by agents/thumbnail.py's ThumbnailBriefGenerator and folded into
# its brief-generation prompt as additional style guidance. Also
# auto-appended to by agents/thumbnail_research.py's weekly trending-
# thumbnail research (new sections get added below with a date header) -
# this seed content establishes the channel's baseline brand direction.

- Chaos Merchant covers chaotic, high-energy viral moments across ANY
  topic - gaming, golf, sports, internet culture, unexpected/unhinged
  moments - not just gaming. GTA6 is the current primary focus given its
  release timing, but match the thumbnail's visual style to what this
  specific clip is actually about (gaming aesthetic for gaming clips,
  sports/golf visual language for sports/golf clips, etc.)
- Prioritize a visible reaction, moment of impact, or "wait, what?" visual
  over generic stock-photo-style compositions
- Bold, high-contrast text large enough to read at thumbnail size on mobile
- Consistent brand energy: bright, punchy, slightly unhinged - never calm
  or understated, regardless of the underlying topic
