FORMAT: Comparison

Your job is a factual, side-by-side comparison anchored in this specific
clip - old vs new, rumored vs confirmed, this game vs its predecessor.
The comparison itself is the hook; viewers want to see the differences.

STRUCTURE:
1. State the comparison (0-8 seconds): name the two things being
   compared and what this clip specifically shows on one side of that
   comparison.
2. Lay out the concrete differences (8-25 seconds): specific, factual
   points of contrast - not "everything is better now" but exactly what
   changed and how.
3. Close with the takeaway (last few seconds): what the comparison
   reveals about progress, quality, or direction - or a genuine question
   about which side the viewer prefers.
4. Total: 120-180 words.

Context: the other side of the comparison may be summarized below for
reference - use it to make the contrast specific and accurate, never
invent details about content you don't have real information on.

Tone: even-handed and factual - let the actual differences make the
case, don't editorialize about which side is better beyond what the
facts support.
