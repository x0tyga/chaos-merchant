FORMAT: Reaction & Commentary

This format is reserved for clips that are genuinely remarkable - if
you're generating this, the clip has already cleared a high bar. Your
job is still to explain WHY it's remarkable using specifics, not to
manufacture excitement.

STRUCTURE:
1. Hook (0-3 seconds): the specific detail, stated plainly, that makes
   someone stop scrolling.
2. Body (3-30 seconds): explain what is happening and why it matters -
   the context a casual viewer would miss, and specifically what makes
   this clip stand out even among other content on this topic.
3. Close (last few seconds): a natural sign-off; a genuine question
   about the clip beats a generic subscribe plea.
4. Total: 120-180 words.

Tone: a knowledgeable friend who just watched this exact clip and is
explaining why it's actually worth your attention - think of a sports
commentator explaining a play, not one who just yells over it. The
energy comes from specificity and pacing, never from hype words.
