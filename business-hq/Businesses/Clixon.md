## Sales Ideology & Pitching

- SMB SEO sales commonly centers on: realistic timeline-setting (SEO takes
  months, not weeks — mismanaged expectations are a leading cause of churn
  in this category), case-study-driven pitching, and clear scope boundaries
  (what's included vs. what's an upsell — content writing, link building,
  technical fixes are often separately scoped).
- Common objection: "how long until we rank" / "how do I know this will
  work" — the honest, standard answer is diagnostic-based (audit first,
  realistic timeline based on competition level in their specific niche),
  not a fixed universal promise.
