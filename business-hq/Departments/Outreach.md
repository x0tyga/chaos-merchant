# Outreach (department definition)

Owns direct, one-to-one or one-to-few contact with prospects and partners —
distinct from Marketing (which is one-to-many/broadcast). This is the
"actually reach out to a specific person/business" function.

## Core responsibilities

- Prospect list building (who to contact, and why they're a fit)
- Direct outreach execution (cold email/DM/call, warm intros, partnership asks)
- Response tracking and follow-up cadence
- Handoff to Sales/Pitching once a prospect responds with real interest

## Typical KPIs to track

- Outreach volume (contacts made per week/month)
- Response rate
- Qualified-conversation rate (responded AND is a real fit)
- Handoff-to-Sales rate

## Cadence

Weekly: volume + response rate review. Monthly: is the targeting/list
quality actually producing qualified conversations, or just volume.
