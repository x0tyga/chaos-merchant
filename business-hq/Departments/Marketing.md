# Marketing (department definition)

Owns how each business gets discovered and how it's perceived once found.
This note defines the ROLE in the abstract — see each business's note for
what Marketing actually means there.

## Core responsibilities

- Positioning: what the business is, who it's for, why it's different
- Content/channel strategy (what gets published, where, how often)
- Brand consistency across every public-facing surface
- Owning the top of the funnel — awareness and initial interest, before
  Sales/Pitching takes over

## Typical KPIs to track

- Reach/impressions per channel
- Engagement rate (CTR, watch time, reply rate — channel-dependent)
- Cost per lead/impression, if paid channels are in play
- Brand-mention sentiment, if tracked

## Cadence

Weekly review of what shipped + what performed; monthly review of
positioning/strategy (is the message still right, has the market moved).
